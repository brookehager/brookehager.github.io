Introduction

Splendor Hair Salon

This business is a hair salon specializing in cut and color for women's hair. The salon offers a luxury experience in a friendly atmosphere. All the hair stylists are certified and experienced professionals. Splendor Hair Salon is a small business in the San Francisco Bay Area serving local customers.

Target audience

The people that use this site are primarily prospective customers. They are likely women or girls who are interested in getting a hair cut and/or color. They want a luxuious and feminine experience while they are getting their hair done by qualified professionals. Visitors also could be returning customers who need to find the location or check the price of a service.

Many of the visitors to the Splendor Hair Salon website are trying checking out the salon. They look at the photos of past clients hair as well as the aesthetics of the website to get an impression of the experience offered there. Other tasks may include: looking at services offered and the prices for each, getting the address, and finding the phone number to make an appointment over the phone.

Services

Our professional stylists will consult with you regarding ideas for your hair, its health, and make suggestions. We can recommend a haircut that will suit your individual face shape, hair type, and lifestyle. Hair is cleansed using the finest of professional shampoos and conditioners selected to benefit your hair and scalp, and a relaxing and thorough scalp massage helps to increase blood flow and release tension. All haircuts include shampoo, cut and style.
